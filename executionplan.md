# DA Flow - Getting Started Guide

This guide will walk you through installing the Chrome Extension and launching the Web Dashboard.

## 1. Load the Chrome Extension

1. Open Google Chrome.
2. Navigate to `chrome://extensions`.
3. Enable **Developer mode** using the toggle in the top-right corner.
4. Click the **Load unpacked** button.
5. Select the `extension` folder from your project directory:
   `/Users/brady/Library/CloudStorage/GoogleDrive-dualsensedad@gmail.com/My Drive/DA Chrome Extension/extension`

The **DA Flow** icon should now appear in your browser's toolbar (you may need to click the puzzle piece icon to pin it).

---

## 2. Access the Web Dashboard

The dashboard is integrated directly into the extension. **No terminal is required.**

1. Click the **DA Flow** icon in your Chrome toolbar to open the popup.
2. Click the **Open Full Dashboard ↗** link at the bottom of the popup.
3. The dashboard will open in a new tab, sharing all data with your tracking extension.

---

## 3. How to Use

### Time Tracking
- **Auto-Detection**: Navigate to `app.dataannotation.tech`. Open the extension, and it will automatically pull the project title from the page breadcrumbs.
- **Start/Stop**: Click the prominent button in the popup to track your time.
- **Emergency Stop**: Press `Cmd+Shift+S` (Mac) or `Ctrl+Shift+S` (Windows) to instantly stop your session without clicking anything. A green checkmark will briefly appear on the extension icon to confirm.
- **Manual Pause**: Toggle the pause button if you need to take a quick break.

### Managing Earnings
- Use the **Open Full Dashboard** button to see all your logged sessions.
- **Set Rates**: In the "Weekly Earnings" tab, click the edit icon (✎) next to a project to input your hourly rate.
- **Track Goals**: Set a daily financial target and watch the progress bar fill as you work.
- **Correction Mode**: Forgot to hit stop? Go to "Session Logs" and click the edit icon to manually adjust your start/end times or active minutes.
- **Mark Reported**: Toggle the "Mark Reported" button on each session once you've submitted your time on the DA site.
